About Us

Welcome to Multi-Modal Emotion Detection, where we pioneer advanced technology to recognize human emotions through multiple input channels. Our system combines facial expressions, voice analysis, and text sentiment to provide comprehensive emotional insights.

Founded in 2023, our team of experts in machine learning, psychology, and human-computer interaction has developed a groundbreaking approach to emotion recognition that achieves higher accuracy by analyzing multiple modalities simultaneously.

Our mission is to enhance human-computer interaction by enabling machines to better understand human emotional states, leading to more empathetic and responsive AI systems.
